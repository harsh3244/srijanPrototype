      What AI Checks

      Clean signals a farmer can understand

      These checks are shown in plain language instead of technical fraud-scoring terms.

      Official website or not

      Checks if the portal uses a real government or bank domain instead of a copy link.

      Fee demand

      Flags unexpected registration fees, file charges, or fast approval payments.

      Document pressure

      Warns when Aadhaar, bank passbook, or OTP is requested too early or in unsafe channels.

      Agent and contact proof

      Looks for branch name, official number, valid office, and verifiable identity details.

      Common Scam Signs
      • Pay money first, then approval later.
      • Send Aadhaar, OTP, or bank details on WhatsApp.
      • Use of urgent words like "today only" or "account blocked".
      • Website link that looks similar, but not exactly official.
      Safe Next Steps
      • Open only official portals or visit the bank/office directly.
      • Ask for branch name, officer name, and written proof.
      • Never share OTP, PIN, or UPI approval blindly.
      • Use AgriShield again if a message looks confusing.
